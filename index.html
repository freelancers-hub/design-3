About Panchajanya Eco Villages

Panchajanya Eco Villages Pvt Ltd, founded by Mr. Rajender Reddy Dasari in 2009, is a trusted name in villa plots and open plot developments. With a strong presence in Hyderabad and other key locations, the company has successfully delivered thousands of plots, growing from its first project of 2,000 plots to developing over 11 lakh square yards.

Built on transparency, responsible pricing, and customer-first values, Panchajanya Eco Villages focuses on creating long-term value for investors and homeowners. For over 25 years, the company has consistently worked towards building sustainable communities that enhance lifestyles and future growth opportunities.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Expert guidance for your real estate investment journey.

What types of plots does Paanchajanya Eco Villages offer?

We offer premium villa plots and open plots in prime locations across Hyderabad (Kadthal, Srisailam Highway), Shirdi, Bangalore, and Bangkok. Our plots are strategically located in emerging growth areas with excellent connectivity and infrastructure.

Are the plots legally approved and RERA registered?

Yes, all our projects are legally approved with clear titles and RERA registration. We ensure complete transparency and provide all necessary documentation including DTCP/HMDA approvals, ensuring a safe and secure investment for our customers.

What amenities are provided in your projects?

Our projects feature modern amenities including wide roads, street lighting, underground drainage, water supply, landscaped parks, security, and gated community features. Each project is designed to provide a comfortable and sustainable living environment.

What are the payment options and financing available?

We offer flexible payment plans to suit your budget, including installment options. We also assist with bank loan arrangements and provide guidance throughout the financing process. Our transparent pricing policy ensures you get the best value for your investment.

Why should I invest in Paanchajanya Eco Villages?

With 25+ years of experience, 50+ successful projects, 527+ acres developed, and 2750+ happy customers, we have a proven track record. Founded by Mr. Rajender Reddy in 2009, we are committed to Quality, Integrity, and Excellence in delivering worthy products at responsible prices.
